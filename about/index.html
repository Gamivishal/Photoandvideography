Yash Raj Motion Picture was founded with a single mission — to tell love stories through the lens of art. Based in Ahmedabad, we have grown into one of Gujarat's most trusted and sought-after visual studios.

Over the years, we have documented more than 700 beautiful events, ranging from intimate home ceremonies to grand 5-day destination weddings. Our style is a perfect blend of documentary photojournalism and fine-art portraiture.

We believe that a great photograph isn't just seen; it's felt. That's why we pour our heart into every frame we capture.

Meet the Artist

Yash Raj

Founder & Lead Photographer

With over a decade of experience behind the lens, Yash Raj has mastered the art of capturing raw, authentic emotion. His journey began with a simple camera and a profound love for storytelling, which quickly evolved into one of Ahmedabad's most prestigious photography studios.

Yash believes that wedding photography is not about staging perfect poses, but about documenting the perfectly imperfect moments that make each love story unique. His fine-art approach ensures that every album feels like a cinematic masterpiece.
